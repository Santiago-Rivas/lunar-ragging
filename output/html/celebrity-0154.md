Rising to fame with her role as Margaery Tyrell in the acclaimed HBO series <i>Game of Thrones</i>, she has established herself as a prominent figure in both independent and mainstream media. Dormer’s compelling acting skills and striking presence have earned her critical praise and a dedicated fanbase. She continues to appear in a variety of projects that showcase her range as an actress.</p>

  <p>Born and raised in Berkshire, England, Dormer developed an early interest in acting during her school years. She attended the Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art in London, where she honed her craft before embarking on her professional career. Her passion for storytelling and dramatic arts motivated her to pursue acting both on stage and in films.</p>
  
  <h2>Career</h2>
  <p>Diplaying her talent across multiple genres, Dormer gained widespread recognition for her role as Queen Margaery Tyrell in <i>Game of Thrones</i>, a performance that earned her numerous award nominations. She transitioned into film with roles in movies such as <i>The Riot Club</i> (2014) and <i>The Forest</i> (2016). More recently, she starred in the Netflix series <i>The Witcher: Blood Origin</i>, expanding her presence in fantasy and action genres. Dormer’s diverse body of work reflects her adaptability and dedication to her craft.</p>
