  <p>Ryan Rodney Reynolds is a Canadian actor, producer, and entrepreneur renowned for his comedic timing and leading roles in a variety of films. Since beginning his acting career in the early 1990s, Reynolds has gained international fame through a series of successful movies, notably his portrayal of Wade Wilson in the Deadpool series, which earned widespread critical and commercial acclaim. In addition to acting, he has involved himself in business ventures and philanthropy, establishing a reputation as a versatile entertainer and savvy investor.</p>
  
  <h2>Early life and education</h2>
  <p>Reynolds was born and raised in Vancouver, British Columbia, the youngest of four children in a household that valued humor and creativity. He developed an interest in acting during his teenage years, participating in local theater productions. He attended Kitsilano Secondary School and later studied at Kwantlen Polytechnic University before pursuing a full-time acting career. His early years included minor television roles that eventually led to more prominent parts in film and television productions.</p>
  
  <h2>Career</h2>
  <p>Ryan Reynolds' rise to fame began with appearances in television series like "Two Guys and a Girl" in the late 1990s. His breakthrough in film came with comedies such as "Van Wilder: Party Liaison" (2002) and "The Proposal" (2009), where his comedic charisma was evident. The turning point of his career was his portrayal of Deadpool in the 2016 film, marking his debut as a lead superhero character. The success of the film led to a franchise and cemented Reynolds as one of Hollywood’s leading actors. He continued to diversify his portfolio with roles in action films, animated features, and streaming service hits like "Free Guy" (2021). Off-screen, Reynolds founded Maximum Effort Productions and invested in various business ventures, including Aviation American Gin, which was later sold for a substantial profit.</p>
  
  <h2>Notable works and projects</h2>
  <p>Reynolds’ notable works include the "Deadpool" films, which broke box office records and became cultural phenomena. Other significant roles include "The Proposal," "Safe House," "6 Underground," and "Free Guy." He also starred in "Detective Pikachu," blending his comedic style with family-friendly content. Reynolds' entrepreneurial endeavors include co-owning Aviation American Gin, which gained popularity through innovative marketing campaigns. His philanthropic efforts focus on health initiatives, disaster relief, and supporting veterans, often utilizing his public platform to promote charitable causes.</p>
  
  <h2>Personal life</h2>
  <p>Ryan Reynolds married actress Blake Lively in 2012, and the couple has four children. Known for his quick wit and sense of humor, he often shares humorous insights about his personal life on social media. Reynolds is passionate about fitness, comic books, and supporting charitable organizations. His personal ethos emphasizes kindness, humor, and giving back. He holds Canadian citizenship and has become a naturalized American citizen as well.</p>
  
  <h2>Awards and honours</h2>
  <p>Reynolds has received several awards, including a star on Canada's Walk of Fame in 2010 and nominations for awards such as the Teen Choice Awards and People's Choice Awards. The success of "Deadpool" earned him awards and nominations in categories recognizing his comedic and leading performances. He has also received recognition for his entrepreneurial ventures and philanthropic work.</p>
  
  <h2>Legacy and impact</h2>
  <p>Ryan Reynolds is regarded as one of Hollywood’s most charismatic and relatable actors, known for blending humor with sincerity. His portrayal of Deadpool redefining the superhero genre had a lasting impact on fan culture and film marketing. Reynolds' approach to business and social engagement has influenced fellow actors to pursue diversified creative endeavors and philanthropic efforts. His popularity continues to grow, making him a prominent figure in entertainment and popular culture.</p>
