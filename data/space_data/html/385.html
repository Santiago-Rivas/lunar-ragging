Alexis Kerry Ohanian is an American entrepreneur, investor, and author best known for co‑founding the social news aggregation website Reddit. Recognized for his influence in the tech industry and startup ecosystem, he has played a pivotal role in fostering online communities and supporting emerging companies. Ohanian is also known for his advocacy on issues related to technology, entrepreneurship, and social justice.

Early life and education

Born and raised in Brooklyn, New York, Ohanian attended Howard University, where he studied commerce and computer science. During his university years, he developed an interest in internet culture and entrepreneurship, which culminated in co‑founding Reddit with Steve Huffman and Aaron Swartz in 2005. His early exposure to technology and digital communities shaped his future career and investment pursuits.

Career

Ohanian's most prominent achievement is the creation of Reddit, which quickly grew into one of the most influential social media platforms facilitating discussions on a wide range of topics. Reddit was acquired by Conde Nast in 2006 and later spun out as an independent company. Following his departure from Reddit's management, Ohanian founded Initialized Capital, a venture capital firm supporting startups like Coinbase and Instacart. He has also written books on technology and entrepreneurship and remains active as an investor and advocate within the tech community.

Notable works and projects

Beyond Reddit and Initialized Capital, Ohanian has contributed to various initiatives promoting internet freedom and diversity in tech. He co‑founded the social impact campaign “Woke Olympics,” and notably, he stepped down from Reddit's board in 2020 to support diversity efforts following protests related to content moderation. His written works include the book Without Their Permission: How the 21st Century Will Be Made, Not Managed, which discusses entrepreneurship and the power of the internet.

Personal life

Ohanian is married to tennis star Serena Williams, with whom he has a daughter. The couple are known for their philanthropy, particularly in education and social justice causes. Ohanian is also passionate about fostering inclusivity in the technology sector. He is a supporter of various social initiatives and frequently discusses the importance of diversity and equity in entrepreneurship and investment.

Awards and honours

Ohanian has received numerous recognitions, including being named to Forbes’ 30 Under 30 list, and he received the National Technology Award from the White House in 2016. His influence in digital innovation and social activism has been widely acknowledged in the tech industry and beyond.

Legacy and impact

As a co‑founder of Reddit, Ohanian helped shape the landscape of social media, fostering online communities and democratizing content sharing. His work in investment and advocacy continues to influence aspiring entrepreneurs, especially those from underrepresented backgrounds. His emphasis on social responsibility within the tech sphere has contributed to ongoing discussions about ethics and inclusivity in digital innovation.
